AILA

Aila, from the Turkish word "aile" meaning "family" is a collection of works depicting the stages and cycles of life. From opening flower buds, baby turtles and deer to tiny insects and fish, the book explores what makes us blessed to be alive and at the same time reflects on the relatively short amount of time smaller creatures have. Photographs of a slaughtered chicken and bloody rope that tied down creatures before meeting their fate accent the works featuring new life.
