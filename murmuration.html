Murmuration

Getting its name for the phenomenon of flocking starlings flying through the air in a group of tens of thousands and the swelling and shrinking motions the flock exhibits. Commissioned by Photoworks, Rinko Kawauchi was quickly drawn to the pier in Brighton, UK. During the winter months, the flocking starlings create a black cloud in the dusk. Captivated by their movements, Rinko Kawauchi continued to explore the flock theme by taking pictures of groups of people as she followed them.
