Cui Cui

Originally presented as a slideshow, Rinko Kawauchi intimately explores her own family in order to bring a feeling of being in one's hometown and the sense that everything is the same as when one left it. Seemingly ordinary events in the works, a woman tending to her garden, pictures of Rinko Kawauchi's grandparents enjoying a walk and a lone pale balloon sitting just above the floor, are used to instill the feeling of meeting and separation and that family memories will always remain alive.
