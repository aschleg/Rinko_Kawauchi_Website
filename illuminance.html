Illuminance

Illuminance was Rinko Kawauchi's first book published outside of Japan and features a collection of 15 years of work. The editing of the book creates a sense of imagination and further exploration into the photographs and message by connecting the works, rather than each acting independently. The works exhibit a sense of timelessness, similar to her other work, giving the viewer a feeling of universality.
