Utatane

Meaning "siesta", Utatane's works are full of dreamlike states that Rinko Kawauchi continued exploring in other works such as the eyes, the ears and AILA. Several of the photographs are noted for young children looking away from the camera in an apparent daydream state while creating a large, perhaps overwhelming, sense of space.
