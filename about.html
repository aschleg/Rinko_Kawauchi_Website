About

Welcome to the Rinko Kawauchi website! My name is Aaron Schlegel (aaron.schlegel at snarchbenimble.com) and I am a huge fan of Ms. Kawauchi's work. I wanted to create the site in hopes of visitors gaining an appreciation for her brilliant and original work. The site was made entirely independently and is more or less a fan site. I hope to continue updating it as new works and news become available. Below are a few other links to sites with information about Rinko Kawauchi. Thank you for taking a look around!
