Semear

Taken in many locations throughout Brazil, Rinko Kawauchi explored the country's scenery and animals, as well as spending time with Japanese-Brazilian communities. Invited by the Mueseu de Arte Moderna of Sao Paulo, she visited Brazil three times during the creation of Semear. Life in Brazil follows the chaos stemming from the seemingly limitless energy of the country, shown by extravagant festivals and wonderous nature, yet it remains a country of contradiction due to the extreme divide between rich and poor.
